Alejandra González Sánchez visited the EVE lab from [Vall d'Hebron University Hospital](https://www.vallhebron.com/en/professionals/alejandra-gonzalez-sanchez), where she's in the lab of Dr. Andrés Antón Pagarolas (a member of ENPEN).

Alejandra spent her time in EVE working on creating a Nextstrain build for CV-A10, and creating Nextclade datasets for CV-A16 and CV-A10. She additionally has been key in helping add Enteroviruses to [Pathoplexus]({{ "/collaborations/pathoplexus/" | relative_url }})!
